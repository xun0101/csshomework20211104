Welcome to

Louise’s Bakery

The Louise’s Bakery is an American-style bakery founded in 2010 to bring the authentic taste of American home baking to New York. With locations in Manhattan New York and after in Brooklyn and Queens NY Area, and also Louise’s Bakery now ships desserts nationwide.


In 2014, Louise’s Bakery original owner passed her oven mitts to Steve and Louise Abrams. Together, the Abrams' have thoughtfully expanded the bakery from its first West Village shop to locations worldwide. Today, Louise’s Bakery can be found in New York City, Los Angeles, Chicago, Honolulu, Mexico City, Seoul, Tokyo, Moscow City, Dubai, Abu Dhabi, Beirut, Kuwait City and Doha. Feel free to browse our website to find out more about our bakery.
